Welcome to Manuals

Modern. Simple. Open Source.
This guide will show you how to use Manuals to manage documentation effectively.

1. Getting Started

Manuals is your central hub for all types of documentation. From internal policies to public help guides, everything is organized in a clear, hierarchical structure that makes finding information effortless.

2. Finding Your Way Around

We provide multiple tools to help you navigate through deep documentation:

  • The Sidebar: On the left side of the manual portal, you'll find the full tree of articles. Workspace articles are public or shared with all staff, while Private articles are just for you.
  • Search: Use the search bar at the top to find keywords in titles or the body of articles.
  • Breadcrumbs: At the top of every page, the breadcrumb trail (e.g., Manuals > Guides > Setup) shows you exactly where you are and lets you jump back to parent folders.
  • Table of Contents: On larger articles, a "On this page" menu appears on the right, allowing you to jump directly to specific sections.

3. Creating Your First Article

Writing documentation should be as easy as writing an email. To start:

  1. Go to the Manuals app in your Odoo dashboard.
  2. Click New.
  3. Add a Title and an Icon (like an emoji) to help users identify the topic.
  4. Use the Rich Text Editor to add content. Use Headings (H2, H3) to structure your text and automatically generate the Table of Contents.
  5. Choose a Parent Article if you want to file this article inside an existing folder.
  6. Author Attribution: The system automatically tracks who created or last updated an article and displays it to readers.
  7. Markdown Support: If you paste raw Markdown (like ## Headers or **bold**), the system automatically detects and compiles it on the frontend!

4. Sharing and Security

You have full control over who sees your documentation:

  • Public Access: Click the Is Published button in the top right of the article form to make it visible to anyone on the internet at /manual.
  • Internal Permissions: Use the Internal Permission setting to choose between "Read Only", "Read & Write" (collaborative), or "No Access" (private).
  • Shared Members: If an article is private, you can still share it with specific people by adding them to the Shared Members list.

5. Multi-Website and Company Support

If your Odoo handles multiple websites or companies, Manuals keeps things organized. You can assign articles to a specific Website or Company, ensuring that users only see the documentation relevant to their current context.

6. Giving Feedback

Help us improve! At the bottom of every public article, you'll find buttons to vote if the article was helpful. This data is collected privately and securely using Postgres procedures to help documentation authors know which guides need more work.

7. Quick Tips

  • Organize by Topic: Use parent articles as folders (e.g., "HR Policies", "Technical Guides").
  • Use Images: A screenshot is often better than a paragraph of text.
  • Links: You can link between articles to create a seamless knowledge web.
  • Direct Name Links: You can share an article instantly by its exact name using the URL /manual/by_name/Your+Article+Name.
