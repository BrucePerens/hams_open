User Websites Portal Feature Documentation

Creating Your Site

Every user is entitled to a personal website. To get started:

  1. Click on your profile name or avatar in the main navigation menu.
  2. If your site isn't set up yet, you'll see a "Create Your Site" button.
  3. Clicking this button will initialize your home page with a default layout.
  4. Use the "Edit" button in the top right to start adding content!

Blogging & Subscriptions

Share your stories and updates with the community through your personal blog.

  • Access your blog at /your-username/blog.
  • Initialize it by clicking "Create Your Blog".
  • New posts can be created directly from the blog index page when you are logged in.
  • Subscriptions: Users can subscribe to your site to receive email notifications when you post new content.

Community Directory

The Community Directory is where you can discover websites created by others.

  • To appear in the directory, go to your Settings and check "Show in Public Directory".
  • The directory shows the total view count for each site, giving you an idea of what's popular.

Content Reporting & Safety

We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for everyone.

  • Report Violation: If you find any content that is inappropriate or violates our guidelines, click the "Report Violation" button at the bottom of the page.
  • Moderation: Our team reviews all reports. Users who repeatedly violate guidelines may receive strikes. After 3 strikes, website features will be suspended.
[@ANCHOR: UX_REPORT_VIOLATION]

Account Appeals

If your account has been suspended, you can submit a one-time appeal through your Portal Dashboard. Explain clearly why you believe the suspension should be lifted.

Your Data & Privacy (GDPR)

You have full control over your data:

  • Data Export: Download all your pages and posts in a JSON format for your own records.
  • Right to Erasure: Permanently delete all your content from our system. Warning: This action is irreversible.

Total View Aggregation

The Community Directory now displays the total view count for each website. This count is aggregated from all public pages and blog posts owned by the user or group.
