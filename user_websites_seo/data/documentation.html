User Websites SEO: Optimization Guide

Optimize your blog visibility without technical complexity.

What is SEO? Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps search engines like Google understand your blog content so it can be shown to the right people.

🚀 Quick Start: Optimizing Your Blog (v1.0.1)

You can manage how your blog appears in search results directly from the "Site" menu on your blog page. This is the easiest way to improve your blog's visibility.

  1. Navigate: Go to your personal blog or your group's blog page (usually at /your-slug/blog).
  2. Open Menu: Locate the Site menu in the Odoo frontend taskbar at the top of the screen.
  3. Select Tools: Click on Optimize SEO. A sidebar or dialog will appear.
  4. Update Info: Fill in the Title and Description. These are the primary snippets that people see when your blog appears in Google search results. Keep the title under 60 characters and description under 160 for best results!
  5. Save: Click the "Save" button in the SEO dialog to apply your changes immediately.

🛠️ Advanced Controls & Backend Management

For more granular control, you can also access SEO settings via your user profile or the group configuration page in the Odoo backend. These settings allow you to fine-tune how search engines and social media platforms interact with your blog.

Managing Your Own SEO

Go to My Profile (click your avatar in the top right, then "My Profile"). Look for the SEO Metadata tab. Here you can edit:

  • SEO Name: A URL-friendly name that helps search engines understand your content structure.
  • Title & Description: The same fields available on the frontend, but managed here for convenience.
  • Keywords: Specific tags that help search engines categorize your blog.

Managing Group SEO

If you are a member of a group, navigate to the User Websites Groups menu. Open your group and select the SEO Metadata tab. Any member of the group can update these settings to help the collective blog grow.

Social Media Optimization (Open Graph)

When you share your blog link on social media (Facebook, X, LinkedIn, etc.), platforms look for "Open Graph" data. You can set a custom Open Graph Image in the SEO settings. This image is what people see in the preview when your link is shared. If you don't set one, your profile avatar will be used by default to ensure you always look professional when shared.

🛡️ Safety & Security

This module is built with privacy and security in mind. It uses a "Zero-Sudo" architecture, which means:

  • Privacy: You only have permission to edit SEO data for your own blog. Our system strictly validates your identity before allowing any changes.
  • Collaboration: Group members can only edit SEO data for their shared group blogs. This allows for team-based management while maintaining strict access control.
  • Protection: We use a microscopic privilege system. This means your SEO settings are isolated. Even if a bad actor tried to use these settings to gain more access, our security system would block them automatically. No broad administrative permissions are granted or required for SEO management.
  • Data Integrity: All SEO changes are logged, and the system ensures that metadata remains consistent with the rest of your website content.
