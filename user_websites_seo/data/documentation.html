Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for Your Blog

This guide explains how to make your personal or group blog more visible to search engines like Google and social media platforms.

Using the "Optimize SEO" Widget

When viewing your blog's main page, you will see a "Site" menu in the top bar (if you are logged in). Click on it and select Optimize SEO.

  • Title: This appears in the browser tab and as the main link in search results. Keep it under 60 characters.
  • Description: A brief summary of your blog. This often appears below the title in search results. Aim for 150-160 characters.
  • Keywords: A comma-separated list of topics your blog covers.

Social Media Previews

You can also control how your blog looks when shared on platforms like Facebook, X (Twitter), or LinkedIn.

  • Preview Image: Upload a custom image that will represent your blog in social media "cards".

Managing SEO in the Backend

If you prefer using the Odoo backend, you can find these settings on your User profile or Group record:

  1. Go to Settings or the User Websites app.
  2. Open your profile or your group's record.
  3. Navigate to the SEO Metadata tab.
  4. Update the fields and click Save.

Security and Privacy

Our "Zero-Sudo" security architecture ensures that you can only edit SEO data for your own content. This keeps your data safe while giving you full control over your blog's visibility.
