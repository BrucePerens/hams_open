Search Engine Optimization (SEO) for Your Website

This guide explains how to make your personal or group website and blog more visible to search engines like Google and social media platforms.

Using the "Optimize SEO" Widget

When viewing your blog's main page, an individual website page, or a blog post, you will see a "Site" menu in the top bar (if you are logged in and own the content). Click on it and select Optimize SEO.

This will open a dialog where you can update the following information:

  • Title: This appears in the browser tab and as the main link in search results. Keep it under 60 characters to ensure it isn't cut off by Google.
  • Description: A brief summary of your blog. This often appears below the title in search results. Aim for 150-160 characters.
  • Keywords: A comma-separated list of topics your blog covers. For example: "cooking, vegan recipes, healthy living".

Social Media Previews

You can also control how your blog looks when shared on platforms like Facebook, X (Twitter), or LinkedIn.

  • Preview Image: Upload a custom image that will represent your blog in social media "cards".

Managing SEO in the Backend

If you prefer using the Odoo backend, you can find these settings on your User profile, Group record, Website Page, or Blog Post:

  1. Navigate to the record you want to edit.
  2. Open the record form.
  3. Look for the SEO Metadata tab or section.
  4. Update the fields and click Save.

Security and Privacy

Our "Zero-Sudo" security architecture ensures that you can only edit SEO data for your own content. This keeps your data safe while giving you full control over your blog's visibility.
