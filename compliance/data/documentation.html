Site Owner's Guide to Regulatory Compliance

A simple guide to help you understand how this website handles privacy and legal rules.

1. Introduction

Running a website comes with legal responsibilities. We've automated the technical parts so you can focus on your community. This guide explains how your site stays legal and what you need to do.

2. Privacy Laws (GDPR & CCPA)

Privacy laws require you to be careful with personal data. Our platform is built to be "Privacy First":

  • Collecting Less Data: We only collect what is strictly needed. Our visitor counters don't track individual people or store their IP addresses.
  • Data Access: Users can download a copy of all their data from their account dashboard at /my/privacy.
  • Right to be Forgotten: Users can delete their account and all their content instantly from the same dashboard.

3. Cookie Consent

In many places, you must ask visitors for permission before using "cookies" (small files that track them).

  • Automatic Banner: This module turns on the standard cookie banner for you. New visitors will see it at the bottom of the page. [@ANCHOR: compliance_post_init_cookie_bar]
  • How to Check: Open your site in an "Incognito" or "Private" window to see the banner yourself.
  • Stay Compliant: If you add new features (like chat or tracking tools), make sure they work with this banner. Don't add tracking code that bypasses it.

4. Legal Documents

Every website needs a few standard documents. We've created basic versions for you:

  • Privacy Policy (at /privacy): Explains how you handle data. [@ANCHOR: compliance_privacy_policy_template]
  • Cookie Policy (at /cookie-policy): Explains the small files your site uses. [@ANCHOR: compliance_cookie_policy_template]
  • Terms of Service (at /terms): The rules for using your site. [@ANCHOR: compliance_terms_of_service_template]

How to Change These Documents

  1. Go to the page on your website (e.g., yourdomain.com/privacy).
  2. Click the Edit button at the top right.
  3. Change the text to fit your needs.
  4. Click Save. Your changes won't be lost when you update the software.

Using Your Own Custom Pages

If you already wrote your own policy pages before installing this module, we won't touch them. We'll hide our version so visitors only see yours. [@ANCHOR: test_compliance_non_destructive_mandate]

If you want to go back to using our version, just delete your custom page or change its address.

5. Accessibility

We've designed our legal pages to be easy to read for everyone, including people who use screen readers or other assistive tools. This helps your site meet "WCAG" accessibility rules.

6. Keeping Your Site Safe (Moderation)

Rules like the "Digital Services Act" require you to handle reports about bad content fairly. Our platform helps with this:

  • Report Button: Visitors can report content that breaks the rules.
  • Privacy for Reporters: We hide the identity of the person who reported the content to prevent retaliation.
  • Fair System: We use a "three-strikes" system for people who break the rules, so everyone is treated fairly.

7. Quick Checklist for Site Owners

  • Check your policies: Read through /privacy and /terms once a month to make sure they still describe your site correctly.
  • Test the banner: Every time you add a new tool or widget, check in a Private window to make sure the cookie banner still works.
  • Review reports: Check the "Content Violations" section in your admin area regularly to handle any reports from users.
