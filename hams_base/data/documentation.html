Base Platform Compliance & Security

Email Policies & Security

  • DMARC Reports: Administrators can view parsed DMARC Aggregate (RUA) reports in the backend to monitor domain email reputation and DKIM/SPF alignment.
  • Configuration Settings: Exposes the required SPF and DMARC DNS records that administrators need to configure for the instance to maintain high email deliverability.
  • Bounce Handling: Automatically alerts system administrators and club officers when emails to members consistently bounce.
  • Account Security Alerts: Automatically sends a security alert to users whenever their login credentials or recovery email address is modified.

Public Portal Features

  • Email Policy Page: A public-facing documentation page (`/email-policy`) detailing the platform's anti-spam stance and email handling policies.
  • Unsubscribe Management: Users can manage their email preferences or completely lock out communications via the `/unsubscribe` and `/unsubscribe/lockout` portals.
