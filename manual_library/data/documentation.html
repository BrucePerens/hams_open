Manual Library User Guide

Version: Odoo 19 Community
Summary: A powerful, easy-to-use knowledge base for your Odoo system.

1. What is Manual Library?

The Manual Library is a place where you can create and organize all your company's instructions, guides, and notes. Think of it like a digital bookshelf where every book is a manual for your team or your customers.

2. Creating Your First Article

To start writing, follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Manuals app from your Odoo home screen.
  2. Click the New button at the top.
  3. Give your article a Title (like "How to use the Coffee Machine").
  4. In the Content tab, start typing your guide. You can use bold text, lists, and even add images.
  5. Click Save when you're done.

3. Organizing with Folders (Hierarchy)

If you have many articles, you can nest them inside each other to stay organized. For example, you can have a "Staff Handbook" article that contains "Safety Rules" and "Vacation Policy" inside it.

  • When creating an article, use the Parent Article field to pick which article it should sit inside.

4. Sharing and Privacy

You can control who sees each article using the Internal Permission setting:

  • Read Only: Everyone in your company can read it, but only admins can change it.
  • Read & Write: Everyone in your company can read and edit it.
  • No Access: Only you and the people you specifically invite can see it.

If you want the whole world (including people not logged in) to see an article on your website, click the Is Published button at the top right of the article form.

5. Viewing on the Website

Once an article is published, anyone can visit /manual on your website to read it. The website version includes:

  • A Search Bar: To quickly find what you're looking for.
  • Table of Contents: A handy list on the side that lets you jump to different sections of long articles.
  • Feedback Buttons: Readers can click "Yes" or "No" to let you know if the article was helpful.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change the order of articles?
Yes! In the list view, you can drag and drop articles using the handle on the left to reorder them.

How do I add an icon to my article?
You can paste an emoji into the small box next to the article title in the editor to make it stand out in the menu.
